Sample Crochet Square

⭐ Beginner • ⏱ 20-30 minutes

🧶 Materials

  • 4mm Crochet Hook
  • Medium Weight Yarn
  • Scissors
  • Yarn Needle

📖 Abbreviations

  • ch = Chain
  • sc = Single Crochet
  • sl st = Slip Stitch
  • FO = Fasten Off

🧵 Pattern

Step 1

Make a slip knot.

Step 2

Chain 16 stitches.

Step 3

Single crochet into the second chain from the hook. Continue until the end of the row.

Step 4

Chain 1, turn your work and repeat until the square reaches the desired size.

💡 Tips

  • Keep your stitches loose if you're a beginner.
  • Count your stitches every row.
  • Practice maintaining even tension.
